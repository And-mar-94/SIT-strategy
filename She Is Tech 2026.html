04 · Key gaps

Three gaps that block long-term value.

Gap 01

Infrastructure debt

Outdated website which doesn't reflect the current scale.

Gap 02

Retention challenge

Our audience (largely from cold email/one-time conference sign-ups) is lukewarm. We need to transition from one-time attendees to active community members.

Gap 03

Visibility gap

Low podcast listenership due to a reliance on short-term social posts rather than “evergreen” engagement.

These gaps limit our ability to scale and create long-term value.
